ABHOUT US 
We are a start-up aiming to establish a Company which caters to fill the void in the area of popcorn making. While there are many companies manufacturing popcorn, we felt the consumers are not presented with an array of flavours and textures that can be blended with the popcorn.
After a lot of trials and research we have come up with varieties that will delight the consumers. We have come up with 4 ranges of popcorn like the luxury range, the gourmet range, the StayFit range, and the classic range. Each range has variety of flavours to offer that will be real treat for a foodie. For the health freaks we are introducing a revolutionary concept of blending whey protein with popcorn. Also since our popcorn are air popped you can binge on it without the guilt. We directly source our corn kernels from reputed organic corn farmers to become a sustainable enterprise.
